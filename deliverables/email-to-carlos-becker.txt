        the LLM never has to do the math
      – /api/fmcsa/verify — proxy that normalizes FMCSA's response into
        a clean eligible / not-eligible decision
      – /api/webhooks/call-completed — sink for post-call extraction
        and classification
      – /api/metrics/* — endpoints powering the dashboard

  • A React dashboard reporting calls by outcome, sentiment mix,
    negotiation-round distribution, agreed-vs-loadboard rate scatter,
    and the most recent calls in a sortable table. Built outside the
    platform per your brief — the goal being to show what an embedded
    operator view looks like end-to-end.

  • All endpoints behind X-API-Key auth, served over HTTPS in the
    deployed environment (Let's Encrypt via a Caddy reverse proxy;
    self-signed locally).
